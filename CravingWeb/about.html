OUR STORY

Born from a love of great food

Cravings started in 2022 when three food lovers realized that finding and ordering from local restaurants was harder than it needed to be. We set out to build a platform that puts restaurants, riders, and customers first — all in one seamless experience. Today, we operate across dozens of cities, empowering small businesses to reach new customers and enabling riders to build a flexible livelihood — all while bringing delicious meals straight to your door.
